Activists Who Stepped Up

Greta Thunberg

Greta Thunberg is a 17-year old from Sweden and is an inspiration for students world wide. She skipped school in order to protest against the Swedish government. She addresses politicians around the world with moving speeches."I should be back in school on the other side of the ocean. Yet you all come to us young people for hope. How dare you! You have stolen my dreams and my childhood with your empty words." This is a quote from Greta as she speaks to the politicians and government officials around the world. Greta has made a huge impact in addressing the need to do something about global warming.

Jerome Foster II

Jerome Foster is a 17-year old who has had a versatile childhood. Jerome started out as an intern for Congressman Jon Lewis. During his internship, Jerome studied the relatonship between economics and climate change. He also joined the rally for climate change every Friday that Greta Thunberg organized. He held daily climate change strikes in front of the White House. Jerome founded OneMillionOfUs which is a non-profit organization that educates young people about global issues. "Now is the time to act" This is a quote from Jerome in the LCV Annual Gala. Jerome has been a vital part of the movement.
